Create and verify PDF accessibility

Acrobat tools make it easy to create accessible PDFs and check the accessibility of existing PDFs.

Adobe Acrobat Pro accessibility checker

A good way to check the accessibility of a document is to use tools that your readers will use. Even if you do not have access to those tools, Adobe Acrobat provides an automated way to check the accessibility of a PDF file.

Create Accessible PDFs and more

A two-part on-demand webinar for anyone learning to create accessible PDFs with Acrobat, along with a discussion of how Adobe supports readability for all.

Acrobat Accessibility series

In this six-part on-demand webinar series, go from the basics of Accessibility to a deep-dive of tagging your PDF files. Each session includes practice files to follow along.
